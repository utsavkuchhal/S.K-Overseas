About Us

At S.K Overseas, tradition is as sacred as creative application. We seek to learn from and be inspired by art and crafts from across the globe. Hence, S.K Overseas maintains a constant encouragement for creative and innovative ideas to be employed in its existing traditional and modern techniques of carpet weaving.

Traditions that have been part of the Indian ethos for centuries, are carried forward and continued at our factories. At S.K Overseas we create complete floor covering solutions that include carpets, durries, rugs, bath rugs, pillows, poufs, wall hangings, storage baskets and floor cushions.

Our consistent perseverance in fulfilling its client’s needs, S.K Overseas is recognized today as an international brand that has skilled employees who work together as one family in the organization’s vast network of factories and offices.

Our Team

We have a small team of 42 people managing various aspects of the companyw with 500+ weavers managing the weaving activities.

Since 2002

We have been exhibiting at International trade fairs since 2004, and are currently exporting to over 50 countries.

Priorities
